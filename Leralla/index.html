Our Purification Process

Our water undergoes a thorough 6-stage purification process:

First, it passes through a meltdown filter to remove particles larger than 10 microns.

Next, it flows through a carbon filter to eliminate any unpleasant tastes and chlorine.

Reverse osmosis filter removes impurities and salts.

1-micron filter for extra fine filtration.

UV light treatment kills bacteria and viruses.

Ozonation for final sterilisation and freshness
