About Leralla Purified Water™

Leralla Purified Water™ is a proudly black-owned Small and Medium Enterprise (SME) based in Pretoria, dedicated to providing high-quality, affordable purified bottled water. Our journey began with a deep concern for the inconsistent water quality and frequent water cuts in Pretoria, which inspired our directors to create a reliable and accessible solution for our community.

We believe clean, safe drinking water should not be a luxury. That’s why we offer premium purified water with custom-branded labels at prices tailored for those who seek value without compromising quality. Using a state-of-the-art 6-stage purification process, we ensure every bottle meets the highest standards of safety and freshness.

Whether for events, businesses, or everyday use, Leralla Purified Water is committed to refreshing our community while promoting affordability, sustainability, and excellence.
